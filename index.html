About

Welcome to a smaller, slower kind of adventure.

Aion's Path is a cozy life-simulation RPG centered on village life, calm exploration, and meaningful personal choices. There is no need to rush: progress comes from time, effort, routine, and the quiet decisions that define where you belong.

This website is the central connection point for everything around the project: the demo, the official wiki, development updates, screenshots, social links, and future community resources.

FAQ

Questions players may ask first.

What is Aion's Path?

A cozy pixel-art life-simulation RPG about village life, routine, exploration, and personal decisions.

Is the game finished?

No. Aion's Path is currently in early development, so features, systems, and content may change over time.

Where can I download the demo?

The current public download link is available through the official Itch.io page.

Does the game have combat?

The current direction focuses on quiet progression, daily life, professions, and exploration rather than combat.
